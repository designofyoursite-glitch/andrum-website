Why this works

This is not about needing less.

When relationship anxiety hits, your body can move faster than your logic. Your heart rate changes. Your breath gets shallow. Your attention locks onto the phone.

The practice does not ask you to talk yourself out of wanting connection. It gives your body a rhythm to follow so there is a little space between the trigger and what you do next.

The goal is not to make you need less. The goal is to help you return to the part of you that can choose.

Andrum

Made for the moment you lose contact with yourself.

Andrum SOS Practices are short guided breathwork audios for the moments when close relationships pull you out of your own center: silence, jealousy, shame, racing thoughts, freeze, anger, and the desperate urge to reach out.

They are not here to erase what you feel. They are here to help you come back to yourself inside it. The free practice is the first one: anxiety after a message or conversation. Start there.

Andrum practices are body-based self-support tools, not therapy, medical care, or crisis support. If breathwork feels uncomfortable, return to natural breathing and pause. If you feel unsafe or at risk of harming yourself, contact local emergency support or a mental health professional.
