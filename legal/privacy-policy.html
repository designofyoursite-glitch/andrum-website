Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Andrum, operated by Mariia Sokolova, an individual entrepreneur registered in Georgia ("Andrum," "we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects personal information when you visit our website, sign up for emails, access free materials, or purchase our recorded audio practices through Teachable.

If you have any questions, contact us at hello@andrum.space.

1. What This Policy Covers

This policy applies to:

  • the Andrum website;
  • Andrum email forms, waitlists, free-practice signups, and marketing communications;
  • purchases of Andrum recorded audio practices made through Teachable;
  • customer support messages and other direct communications with us.

Teachable operates its own platform and checkout experience. When you use Teachable, Teachable may process your information under its own policies as well as in connection with our products.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you interact with Andrum, we may collect:

  • Contact information, such as your name and email address.
  • Purchase information, such as the product purchased, purchase date, and access status.
  • Support information, such as messages you send us and information needed to respond.
  • Technical information, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring URLs, and approximate location.
  • Marketing preferences, such as whether you subscribed to or unsubscribed from emails.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive health information. Please do not send us medical records, diagnoses, or private health details by email or through website forms.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information when you:

  • sign up for a free practice, waitlist, newsletter, or email sequence;
  • purchase or access an Andrum product through Teachable;
  • contact us for support;
  • visit or interact with our website;
  • click links in our emails or website, if tracking tools are enabled.

At the time this policy was prepared, the Andrum website does not intentionally use advertising pixels or analytics trackers. We may add analytics, advertising, or conversion tracking tools later. If we do, we will update this policy and, where legally required, ask for cookie or tracking consent.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to:

  • provide access to free and paid Andrum products;
  • process purchases and customer support requests;
  • send emails you signed up to receive;
  • send product updates, educational or marketing emails, and launch announcements;
  • improve our website, products, and customer experience;
  • understand what content and offers are useful to our audience;
  • protect our website, business, users, and legal rights;
  • comply with legal, tax, accounting, and platform obligations.

5. Legal Bases for Processing

If privacy laws such as the GDPR or UK GDPR apply to your information, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: to provide products, account access, support, and purchase-related communications.
  • Consent: for optional email marketing, non-essential cookies, and tracking where consent is required.
  • Legitimate interests: to operate and improve our website, respond to inquiries, prevent fraud, and understand business performance.
  • Legal obligations: to keep records required for tax, accounting, compliance, or legal purposes.

You may withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

6. Email Marketing

If you subscribe to our emails, we may send you free practices, product updates, educational content, launch emails, and promotional messages.

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or contacting hello@andrum.space.

We do not want anyone on our list who does not want to be there.

7. Purchases Through Teachable

Andrum products are sold and delivered through Teachable. When you purchase through Teachable, Teachable may collect and process information such as your name, email address, payment details, billing details, tax information, device data, and course access data.

Payment information is processed by Teachable and/or its payment providers. We do not directly receive or store full payment card numbers.

Please review Teachable's legal policies for more information about how Teachable handles your data.

8. Cookies And Tracking Technologies

Cookies and similar technologies may be used to:

  • make the website or checkout function correctly;
  • remember preferences;
  • understand website traffic and performance;
  • measure email, advertising, or conversion activity, if such tools are enabled later.

Some cookies are necessary for the website or checkout to work. Others, such as analytics or advertising cookies, may require your consent depending on your location.

For more detail, see our Cookie Policy.

9. How We Share Information

We may share personal information with trusted service providers who help us operate Andrum, including:

  • Teachable, for product hosting, checkout, and access;
  • email marketing providers;
  • website hosting and domain providers;
  • analytics, advertising, or conversion tools, if enabled;
  • payment processors;
  • professional advisers, such as accountants, lawyers, or compliance consultants;
  • legal or government authorities when required by law.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary meaning of selling a customer list for money. If we later use advertising tools that may be considered "sharing" or "sale" under certain privacy laws, we will update this policy and provide any required choices.

10. International Data Transfers

Andrum is operated from Georgia and sells worldwide. Our service providers may process information in the United States, European Economic Area, United Kingdom, Georgia, or other countries.

If your information is transferred internationally, we will use reasonable safeguards required by applicable law where they apply.

11. How Long We Keep Information

We keep personal information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, including:

  • providing product access and support;
  • maintaining business, tax, and accounting records;
  • complying with legal obligations;
  • resolving disputes;
  • enforcing agreements;
  • maintaining email preferences and unsubscribe records.

When information is no longer needed, we will delete it or anonymize it where reasonably possible.

12. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • access the personal information we hold about you;
  • correct inaccurate information;
  • request deletion;
  • object to or restrict certain processing;
  • withdraw consent;
  • request a copy of your information;
  • opt out of certain marketing, sale, sharing, or targeted advertising uses if applicable.

To make a privacy request, contact hello@andrum.space.

We may need to verify your identity before completing certain requests.

13. California, EU, UK, And Other Regional Rights

If you are located in California, the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or another region with specific privacy laws, you may have additional rights under local law.

Andrum is a small business, and some privacy laws apply only when specific thresholds are met. Even where a law does not technically apply, we aim to handle personal information with care, transparency, and respect.

14. Children's Privacy

Andrum products and website are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact hello@andrum.space and we will take appropriate steps.

15. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal information. No website, platform, email system, or online transmission is completely secure.

Please use care when sharing personal information online.

16. Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date shows when the current version was prepared.

If we make material changes, we may provide notice through the website, email, or another reasonable method.
