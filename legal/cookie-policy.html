This Cookie Policy explains how Andrum may use cookies and similar technologies on its website. Andrum is operated by Mariia Sokolova, an individual entrepreneur registered in Georgia.

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies include pixels, tags, local storage, SDKs, tracking links, and device identifiers.

These technologies can help a website function, remember preferences, understand performance, or measure marketing activity.

2. Current Website Setup

At the time this policy was prepared, the Andrum website does not intentionally use advertising pixels or analytics trackers.

Visitors who click a purchase button are redirected to Teachable checkout. Teachable may use cookies and similar technologies on its own platform under Teachable's policies.

Andrum may add analytics, advertising, email measurement, or conversion tracking tools later. If we do, this policy should be updated before or at the time those tools are added.

3. Types Of Cookies We May Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are needed for a website, form, checkout, security feature, or account access to work. They cannot usually be turned off without breaking core functionality.

Preference Cookies

These cookies remember choices such as language, region, or display preferences.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how people use the website, which pages are visited, and what content or offers are useful.

Marketing And Advertising Cookies

These cookies or pixels may help measure campaigns, build audiences, show relevant ads, or understand whether someone purchased after seeing an ad.

At the time this policy was prepared, Andrum does not intentionally use these on the website. If added later, consent may be required depending on your location.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party platforms may use their own cookies or similar technologies when you interact with them.

This may include:

  • Teachable, when you visit checkout or access purchased products;
  • email marketing providers, when you sign up or click email links;
  • analytics providers, if enabled later;
  • advertising platforms, if enabled later;
  • embedded media or other tools, if added later.

We do not control all cookies set by third-party services.

5. Consent

In some regions, including the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom, non-essential cookies and tracking technologies generally require consent before they are used.

If Andrum adds non-essential analytics, advertising pixels, or similar tracking tools, we should provide a cookie banner or consent mechanism where required.

You should be able to reject non-essential cookies as easily as accepting them where applicable law requires that choice.

6. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or limit cookies.

If you block all cookies, some website, checkout, account, or product access features may not work properly.

If Andrum adds a cookie consent banner later, you may also be able to manage choices through that banner.

7. Updates To This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when our website tools, analytics, advertising, or checkout setup changes.

The "Last updated" date shows when the current version was prepared.
